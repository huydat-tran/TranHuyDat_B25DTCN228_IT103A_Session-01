T1 WORLD CHAMPION 2025

Hình ảnh T1 vô địch chung kết thế giới 2025

The League of Legends 2025 World Championship has concluded, crowning T1 as the best team in the world once more. Let's take a look at all of the possible champions T1 players can choose for a T1 Worlds 2025 skin, as they become the first back-to-back-to-back World Champions. In the Worlds 2025 Grand Final, T1 faced fellow LCK team KT Rolster in the highest-stakes Telecom War in League of Legends history. After five beautiful games of League of Legends, T1 successfully completed the first three-peat in LoL Esports history.
